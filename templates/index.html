Sutherland-Hodgman Algorithm

This visualization demonstrates the Sutherland-Hodgman polygon clipping algorithm, which clips a subject polygon against a convex clipping polygon.

Each edge of the clipping polygon is processed in sequence, determining which vertices of the subject polygon should be kept or discarded.
